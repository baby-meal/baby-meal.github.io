BabyMeals

Privacy-first and ad-free baby meal timer app for iOS. Control the timer with a single touch or using your voice with Siri.

Keep an overview on meal times and frequency to understand your baby's food needs better. All data is securely stored only on your device.

Our Privacy Policy

The BabyMeals app is designed from the ground up to protect your and your baby's information. It doesn’t gather your personal information to sell to advertisers or other organizations. BabyMeals requires no user registration and does not contain 3rd-party products and services like analytics or advertising networks. These may use your information to serve such advertising.

BabyMeals is build upon Apple's development platform for iPhone and iPad. Information collected by Apple is governed by their privacy policy. We encourage you to learn about their privacy practises as you install BabyMeal or other apps.

Please contact us, if you have any questions or concerns about BabyMeal's Privacy Policy.
